RAI - Receipt and expense management app

📋 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

RAI Expense Manager collects the following information:

  • Email address - Only if you create an account via Google or email
  • Username - To personalize your experience
  • Receipts and expenses - Stored locally on your device only
  • Receipt photos - Only if you import them

2. How We Use Your Data

  • ✅ Authentication and account security (Firebase)
  • ✅ Sending marketing emails ONLY with your explicit consent (checkbox)
  • ✅ App improvement via anonymous analytics
  • ✅ Displaying ads (AdMob) - unless you are premium

3. Data Storage

  • 🔒 Receipt data: Only on your phone (local Hive storage)
  • ☁️ Firebase: Authentication and user accounts
  • 📧 Google Sheets: Marketing email collection (optional)
  • 📊 Google AdMob: Ads and analytics

4. Third-Party Services Used

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) - Account management
  • Google AdMob - Advertising
  • Google Sheets - Marketing email collection (consent required)
  • Google ML Kit - Text recognition on receipts (100% on-device)

5. Advertising

The app displays ads via Google AdMob:

  • 📱 Banners at the bottom of the "Expenses" screen
  • 🎜 Interstitial ads on the "Reports" tab
  • ⭐ Premium users have no ads

6. Marketing Consent (Email)

We collect your email to send you:

  • 📧 App updates
  • 💡 Usage tips
  • 🎁 Promotional offers (rare)

⚠️ IMPORTANT: You must manually check the box on the welcome screen. Without your explicit consent, no email is collected or sent.

You can unsubscribe at any time by contacting us.

7. Deleting Your Data

You can delete all your data in several ways:

  • 🗑️ Uninstall the app (deletes all local receipts)
  • 📧 Contact us to delete your marketing emails
  • 🔐 Sign out and delete your Firebase account

➡️ Complete account deletion procedure

8. Data Security

  • 🔐 Encrypted transmission (HTTPS) to Firebase and Google Sheets
  • 📱 Receipt data stored locally only
  • 🔒 No cloud storage of receipts or expenses
  • 🛡️ Compliant with Google Play security requirements

9. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • ✔️ Access your data
  • ✔️ Rectify your information
  • ✔️ Delete your account and data
  • ✔️ Withdraw your marketing consent
